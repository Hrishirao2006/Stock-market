- Introduction to F&O:
  so trading happens in 3 different ways- (Futures, Options and normal trading)
  Futures: futures was introduced so that people could save thier capital from extreme events. Max duration of a futures is 3 months.
  options: On 20th july, we looked at options of 21st july for both call and put at the level of 24,200.
  Call is when you predict that the market will go up and PUT is when u predict the market will go down
  Thing to be cleared: Call Buy is when u bet that the market is going up and Call sell is when u r predicting that the market is going to go down whereas put buy is when u predict that the market is going to go down and put sell is when u predict the market is going to go up
  under options, sell has less rick due to a factor known as theta decay.
  the concept of theta decay hasnt been fully clarified but in informal terms, it is the factor by which the value of the premium comes down as expiry is approached.
under option trading, it is never advised to keep a buy trade open for long durations due to theta decay.
