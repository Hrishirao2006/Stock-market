# Fibonacci series
(Note: this is explained by NIFTY 50 but can be applied in any exchange)
how to trade
fibonacci ratio: 6.1 golen ratio, week, daily, hourly 
fibonacci sequence: 1st ratio: 23.6%, 2nd- 38.2% 3rd- 50%, 4th- 61.8% (golen ratio), 5th- 78.6%, 6th- 100%, 7th- 123.6%
(Note: mark the open, high, low, close of everyday market )
21st july markings: open- 24,216; high- 24,262; low- 24,135; close- 24,187 
step 1: Take the difference of high and low
24,262 - 24,135 = 127
apply the Fibonacci series to this difference and add the lower price with it
(127* 23.6%) + 24,135= 24,164
(127* 38.2%) + 24,135 = 24,183
(127 * 50%) + 24,135 = 24,198
(127* 61.8%) + 24,135 = 24,213 (golen ratio)
(127 * 78.6%) + 24,135 = 24,234
(127*100%) + 24,135 = 24,262

from these levels, we understand that if the market is sitting above the golen ratio, i.e, if it is sitting at 1st, 2nd, 3rd then we say that the market is bearish and we set the market for a put buy or call sell. 
today around 12:00 p.m the market was sitting around 23,999 so from this we understand that the market was acting highly bearish. 
in these cases we set the level at the golden ratio as the stop loss
